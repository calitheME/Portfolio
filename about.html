About Me

Engineering Related

I am Calista Kirchhoff, also known as Cali, an engineering student at the University of Oklahoma (OU) majoring in Mechanical Engineering and minoring in Electrical and Computer Engineering.

In the past, I worked as an unlicensed A&P aircraft technician where I developed my love for aviation and hands-on work. Since then, I have joined AIAA's (American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics) DBF (Design, Build, Fly) team at OU, where I am continuing this journey. I also had the opportunity to attend SCR's (Sooner Competitive Robotics) onboarding and plan to continue working with them in the future.

During summer of 2025, ONEOK brought me in as a first-time intern. Through that experience, it became clear that there is still so much to learn. Today, I am striving to learn the technical skills that I believe will help me help you next summer, 2026. This website is allowing me to show my progress. Feel free to save URL to follow along.
